ABOUT THE SORORITY

PHILANTHROPY

Alpha Chi Omega supports the fight against domestic violence

  • Our Cause
  • Our philanthropic goal as a chapter is to raise funds for the local women's shelter in Champaign- Courage Connection, and strive to spread awareness for the issue of domestic violence. Each semester we hold events to help support the shelter that gives so many women a safe place to stay. This past fall, our chapter hosted a golf tournament, in which fraternities signed up teams to play. In the spring, we hosted our yearly Mac N' Cheese dinner at the chapter house. This year, the dinner raised $7,653 for Courage Connection. We hope you can see why our cause is so close to our hearts, and that it may inspire you to become a part of it.
